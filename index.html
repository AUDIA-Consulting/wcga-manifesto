Join the Movement
Core Belief

Gymnastics provides the foundation for developing daring, strong, and confident women.

This sport transforms young girls into exceptional leaders who shape industries, strengthen communities, and inspire generations. The impact extends far beyond the competition floor: families benefit from shared commitment and values, businesses gain principled and driven leaders, and communities are enriched by women who lead with confidence and integrity.

At an individual level, gymnastics instills habits of hard work, perseverance, sacrifice, and delayed gratification—qualities that are increasingly rare, yet essential for long-term personal fulfillment and professional success.

Our Foundation

Purpose

To champion the growth and promotion of women's collegiate gymnastics.

We exist to elevate this sport to its rightful place as the premier women's collegiate sport. Through advocacy, innovation, and collaboration, we create opportunities for female student-athletes to excel academically and athletically, and to become leaders who will transform their worlds.

Vision

Women's collegiate gymnastics is recognized as the premier women's collegiate sport, filling arenas with passionate fans and creating transformative opportunities for student-athletes.

We see a future where the Olympic passion that makes gymnastics the number one watched sport globally translates into year-round engagement collegiately. Where every meet at every level is an electric experience.

Mission

To strengthen and expand the reach of women's collegiate gymnastics, establishing it as the leading female collegiate sport through connection, empowerment, and education.

How We Achieve This

  • Advocating for resources and recognition at every level
  • Innovating how we present, promote, and grow the sport
  • Building a collaborative community where every program's success elevates all programs
  • Protecting our athletes and the integrity of our sport
  • Demonstrating that investing in women's gymnastics creates impact far beyond competition
This is more than an association. This is a movement.
Our Commitment

The Values That Drive Us

Leadership

We Lead by Example

You lead by example, demonstrating the standard you want others to follow. You contribute actively to decision-making, bringing your expertise to shape our collective direction. We show initiative and don't wait for perfect conditions—we identify what needs to happen and make it happen.

We lead our institutions. We lead our conferences. We lead the nation in proving what women's collegiate sport can become.

Innovation

We Pioneer the Future

You are a pioneer, constantly seeking ways to elevate the sport through imaginative thinking and bold action. You actively seek solutions to existing challenges, leveraging technology and fresh perspectives.

The premier women's collegiate sport won't be built by doing what's always been done. We innovate in how we present our sport, engage our fans, develop our athletes, and tell our story.

Grit

We Persevere Through Challenge

You inspire others with your commitment to excellence, regardless of obstacles. You embrace a growth mindset, viewing setbacks not as failures but as essential steps toward mastery.

Building the premier women's collegiate sport won't be easy. Grit is what separates programs that talk about growth from programs that achieve it. We don't just endure challenges; we use them as fuel.

Connection

We Strengthen Through Unity

You communicate openly and respectfully, ensuring others feel heard, valued, and included. You collaborate willingly, seeking opportunities to support others and strengthen our collective impact.

We are stronger together. A program that succeeds and then lifts others creates momentum that benefits everyone. We build genuine relationships and honor those who came before us.

Stewardship

We Protect What Matters

You protect the well-being of every athlete, ensuring they feel supported, safe, and valued. You use resources wisely, making decisions that strengthen both present success and future growth.

We are stewards of something precious: the dreams of young women, the traditions built by programs before us, and the potential of a sport that can inspire millions.

Passion

We Ignite Enthusiasm

You bring energy and enthusiasm to everything you do, inspiring others through your example. You stay motivated even when challenges arise, demonstrating an unwavering commitment to our mission.

Passion is contagious. Our passion fills arenas. Our passion attracts sponsors. Our passion transforms casual observers into lifelong fans.

The Moment Is Now

The Opportunity Before Us

Women's collegiate gymnastics is the female equivalent of football on the male side. We have everything required to become the premier women's collegiate sport.

World-Class Athleticism

Combined with artistic expression. Athletes who have dedicated thousands of hours to their craft emerge as disciplined, resilient, and focused individuals ready to excel in any arena.

Unmatched Fan Experience

The energy in a packed gymnastics arena is electric. The pageantry rivals any collegiate event. Fans don't just watch; they participate—creating an atmosphere that's intimate yet explosive.

Student-Athletes Who Do It All

Our gymnasts maintain exceptional GPAs while training 20+ hours per week. They lead student organizations, volunteer in communities, and graduate ready to make an immediate impact.

Proven Global Appeal

Olympic women's gymnastics is the number one most-watched sport globally during the Games. The audience exists. The appetite is undeniable. Our opportunity is to channel that passion into sustained growth.

The question isn't whether we can become the premier women's collegiate sport. The question is: will we invest what it takes to claim that position?
Commitment Required

The Investment Required

This vision demands more than passive membership. It requires active investment from every program, every coach, every administrator who believes in what we're building.

Investment in Advocacy

Using your voice to elevate the sport at your institution, in your conference, and in the national conversation. Highlighting the academic excellence, leadership development, and commercial potential of women's gymnastics.

Investment in Innovation

Pioneering new approaches to fan engagement, athlete development, and sport promotion. Embracing technology, creative choreography, and fresh perspectives that attract audiences beyond the traditional gymnastics world.

Investment in Collaboration

Recognizing that our collective success elevates every program. Sharing knowledge, resources, and best practices—supporting fellow members not as competitors but as partners in building something bigger.

Investment of Belief

Committing to the long-term vision even when challenges arise. Showing up when it matters. Leading by example. Refusing to accept that women's gymnastics should be anything less than the premier women's collegiate sport.

Investment of Revenue & Resources

Raising money and allocating the financial capital to operate at a premier level. Prioritizing investment in marketing budgets and staffing structures that mirror the elite status of the sport.

This is the investment that transforms our association into a movement.
